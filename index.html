01 / The studio

A floors & walls studio, built for scale and craft.

Raw Surfaces works at two ends of the same trade. For builders and developers, we grind, prepare and coat large commercial and industrial floors. For architects, designers and homeowners, we lay polished concrete, microcement and Venetian plaster through entire homes — floors and walls.

What we don’t do is small, disconnected jobs. We take on whole surfaces and whole projects, and every one is led personally by Brayden Warhurst — no outsourcing, no underskilled crews.

11 / FAQ
Do you do commercial and industrial work?

Yes — large-scale concrete grinding, surface preparation, epoxy/resin systems and large-format polished concrete for warehouses, retail, hospitality and industrial floors. Builders and developers across Melbourne, Geelong and Victoria are a core part of our work.

Do you take small jobs?

We focus on whole floors and walls and larger-scale projects rather than one-off small jobs. We’ll happily handle surface prep, a benchtop or a feature element when it’s part of a project we’re already doing, or when the scale is right.

Do you do whole homes?

Yes. For high-end residential we lay polished concrete, microcement and Venetian plaster across all the floors and walls — designed and delivered as one cohesive project.

Who does the work, and how do I get a quote?

Every project is led personally by Brayden Warhurst, the owner, with 14+ years on the tools — never outsourced. Book a consultation for a fixed, transparent quote: call 0468 934 409 or email brayden@rawsurfaces.com.au.
